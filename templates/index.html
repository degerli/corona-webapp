This web application is based on a novel deep learning architecture which is a combination of CovNets and Depthwise separable CovNets, trained to recognise Corona Virus from chest x-ray images. We are constantly working to increase its accuracy and robustness with proper validation.

Please note that the predictive accuracy of the model, depends on the quality and quantity of the training data and there is a very scarce availability of COVID 19 affected chest x ray images. Therefore the model has limitations and cannot be used for definite clinical predictions! Instead it can be used for research and comparison purposes.

But we are constantly updating images dataset available on github as per the initiative of this git hub repoiee8023 repowe are retraining our model with the availability of images to make it as accurate as possible and in the next phase we will be adding a functionality to classify COVID 19, SARS, Bacterial infections and Normal cases.

  • The virus is only spreading in colder weather countries  It is not true. It can spread in any type of climate. Relatively warmer south east asian countries like Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand and Phillipines recorded highest rate of infection so far.

Several people have been cured until now. As per the scientists, coronavirus is a mild type of virus infection with only 2 per cent fatality rate.
