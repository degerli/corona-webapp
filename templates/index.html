{% extends "base.html" %} {% block content %} This web application is based on a novel deep learning architecture which is a combination of CovNets and Depthwise separable CovNets, trained to recognise Corona Virus from chest x-ray images. The neural network achieved accuracy exceeding 98% on the relevant test set. Please note that the predictive accuracy of the model, depends on the quality and quantity of the training data and there is a very scarce availability of COVID 19 affected chest x ray images. Therefore the model has limitations and cannot be used for definite clinical predictions! Instead it can be used for research and comparison purposes. But we are constantly updating images dataset available on github as per the initiative of Joseph Paul Cohen. Postdoctoral Fellow, Mila, University of Montreal The 2019 novel coronavirus (COVID-19) presents several unique features. While the diagnosis is confirmed using polymerase chain reaction (PCR), infected patients with pneumonia may present on chest X-ray and computed tomography (CT) images with a pattern that is only moderately characteristic for the human eye Ng, 2020. While PCR tests offer many advantages they are physical things that require shipping the test or the sample. X-ray machines can be plugged in to screen patients as long as they have electricity. Imagine a future where we run out of tests and then the majority of radiologists get sick. AI tools can help general practitioners to triage and treat patients. Companies are developing AI tools and deploying them at hospitals. This is our sincere attempt to build AI based diagnostic tool which will help in diagnosing novel coronavirus in remote locations and where medical resources are scarce. We are constantly updating our model and retrain our model with the availability of images and in the next phase we are also developing AI based diagnostic tool which can classify COVID 19, SARS, Bacterial infections and Normal cases.

  • Wearing the mask will protect from the virusMasks are for people infected with the virus,not for a healthy individual.

  • The virus is only spreading in colder weather countriesIt is not true. It can spread in any type of climate. Relatively warmer south east asian countries like Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand and Phillipines recorded highest rate of infection so far.
  • Corona Virus is the most dangerous virusThere are a plethora of different kinds of viruses out there in the world, which is more deadlier than COVID-19.Coronavirus is a fatal disease, but not every person who gets infected with this virus dies. Several people have been cured until now. As per the scientists, coronavirus is a mild type of virus infection with only 2 per cent fatality rate.
